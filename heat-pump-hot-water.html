Heat Pump Hot Water Systems

Efficient hot water for Victorian homes and businesses.

Heat pump hot water systems use electricity efficiently to heat water, making them a strong alternative to traditional gas or electric hot water systems. They can reduce energy use and work well with solar power.

Benefits

  • Lower running costs compared to traditional electric systems
  • Good option for electrifying the home
  • Can pair with solar panels
  • May be eligible for energy upgrade incentives
  • Reduces reliance on gas
Eligible Victorian customers may access upfront discounts through approved energy upgrade programs. Final eligibility, product suitability and pricing are subject to site assessment, program requirements and provider approval.
