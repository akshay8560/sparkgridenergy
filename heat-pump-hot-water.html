Heat Pump Hot Water Systems

Efficient hot water for Victorian homes and businesses.

Heat pump hot water systems use electricity efficiently to heat water and can be a strong alternative to traditional gas or electric hot water systems.

Benefits

  • Lower energy use compared to traditional electric systems
  • Good for home electrification
  • Can pair well with solar panels
  • Reduces reliance on gas
  • May qualify for energy upgrade incentives

The Victorian Government VEU program says eligible customers can save on upfront costs and annual energy bills when upgrading to a heat pump water heater. Solar Victoria also offers hot water rebates for eligible heat pump and solar hot water products.

Eligible Victorian customers may access upfront discounts through approved energy upgrade programs. Final eligibility, product suitability, discount amount and pricing are subject to site assessment, program requirements and provider approval.
