Reverse-Cycle Air Conditioning Installation

Efficient heating and cooling for Victorian homes and businesses.

Reverse-cycle air conditioners provide both heating and cooling. They are a popular option for Victorian households looking to move away from old gas heaters, electric resistance heaters or inefficient air conditioners.

System Types

System TypeExplanation
Single split systemOne indoor unit connected to one outdoor unit
Multi-split systemMultiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit
Ducted reverse-cycle systemWhole-home heating and cooling through ductwork

What Size Split System Do I Need?

AreaCommon System Size
Small bedroom2.5 kW
Medium bedroom3.5 kW
Living room5.0 kW
Large living / open plan7.1 kW
Very large open area8.0–9.0 kW
Final sizing depends on room size, insulation, ceiling height, windows, layout and installation conditions. A qualified installer or provider must confirm the correct system size before installation.
