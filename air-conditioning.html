What Could Go Wrong With Other Installers?

Choosing an unqualified or non-compliant installer can cost you significantly more than the rebate is worth.

⚠️

Wrong System Size

A quick, no-assessment quote can leave you with an undersized or oversized unit — locking you into high bills and poor comfort for years.

❌

VEU Non-Compliance

If the model or installation isn't VEU-compliant, the discount can be denied and you may be required to pay the full retail price after installation.

🔧

Poor Installation Quality

Incorrect placement, exposed wiring and messy finishes are problems you'll see — and potentially pay to fix — every single day.

💸

Hidden Charges

Admin fees, site extras and surprise upgrades that only appear once the job has already started can turn a discounted deal into a costly one.
