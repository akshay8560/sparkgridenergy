Reverse-Cycle Air Conditioning Upgrades

Efficient heating and cooling for Victorian homes and businesses.

SparkGrid Energy helps Victorian households and businesses upgrade to efficient reverse-cycle air conditioning systems. These systems provide both heating and cooling, making them a smart replacement for old gas heaters, electric resistance heaters or outdated air conditioners.

System Types

System TypeMeaning
Single split systemOne indoor unit connected to one outdoor unit
Multi-split systemMultiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit
Ducted reverse-cycle systemWhole-home heating and cooling through ducts

What Size Split System Do I Need?

AreaCommon System Size
