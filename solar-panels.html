Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs)

Solar systems may be eligible for Small-scale Technology Certificates, commonly applied as an upfront discount. Most system owners assign the right to create STCs to an agent in exchange for a discount or delayed cash payment. The value depends on system size, location, eligibility, installation date and STC market value.
