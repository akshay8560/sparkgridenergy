How Our Process Works

A simple, transparent path from enquiry to installation.

1

Submit an Enquiry

Customer fills out form with property details, current system and upgrade interest.

2

Eligibility Check

SparkGrid checks basic eligibility and asks for photos or extra details if required.

3

Product and Site Assessment

A qualified installer or provider assesses the property, system size, installation complexity and product suitability.

4

Quotation

Customer receives a clear quote showing product, installation scope, estimated discount and customer payable amount.

5

Installation

Licensed professionals complete the installation, decommissioning and required compliance work.

6

Documentation

Customer receives invoice, warranty information, compliance documents and program forms where applicable.

7

Post-Installation Support

SparkGrid follows up after installation to ensure the customer understands the system and has received the required documents.
