Building-Based LED Lighting Upgrade

Efficient interior lighting for commercial and industrial facilities.

Retrofit your facility's interior lighting with state-of-the-art LED fixtures to improve illumination, reduce maintenance, and cut energy consumption.

Ideal For

  • Office buildings and commercial interiors
  • Warehouses and industrial facilities
  • Indoor car parks and basements
  • Retail and hospitality spaces

Benefits

  • Lower electricity costs compared with older fluorescent or halogen lighting
  • Longer lamp life and reduced maintenance
  • Brighter, more consistent light quality
  • May be eligible for energy upgrade incentives depending on the project
Eligible Victorian customers may access upfront discounts through approved energy upgrade programs. Final eligibility, product suitability, discount amount and pricing are subject to site assessment, program requirements and provider approval.
