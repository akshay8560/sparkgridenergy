Rebates & Eligibility Disclaimer

Eligible Victorian customers may access upfront discounts through approved energy upgrade programs. Final eligibility, product suitability and pricing are subject to site assessment, program requirements and provider approval.

Important Information

  • Discounts and rebates are not guaranteed for all customers
  • VEU discounts depend on the system being replaced, product installed, size and site conditions
  • Minimum customer co-payments apply under VEU program rules
  • STC discounts for solar depend on system size, location and market value at time of installation
  • Final VEEC calculation and quotation must be confirmed by the nominated VEU Accredited Provider

What We Do Not Claim

SparkGrid Energy does not represent that upgrades are free, fully government-funded, or available to all customers without assessment. We provide clear information to help customers understand their options.

SparkGrid Energy provides sales, coordination and customer support for energy upgrade services. Final eligibility, product approval, installation requirements, rebates, discounts and certificate creation are subject to assessment by the relevant accredited provider, installer and program rules.
