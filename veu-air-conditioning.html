VEU Air Conditioning Discounts in Victoria

Understand how the Victorian Energy Upgrades program may reduce the cost of your aircon upgrade.

Eligible Victorian households and businesses may access upfront discounts through the Victorian Energy Upgrades program when installing approved high-efficiency reverse-cycle air conditioners.

The VEU discount depends on the existing heating or cooling system being replaced, the new product installed, the product size, site conditions and program eligibility.

Discounts are not guaranteed. Final eligibility, product approval, VEEC calculation and quotation must be confirmed by the nominated VEU Accredited Provider before installation.

Minimum Co-Payment

Minimum customer co-payments apply under the VEU program. For many non-ducted split systems below 10 kW, the minimum co-payment starts from $200 per installed product. For ducted systems, multi-split systems and larger systems, higher co-payment requirements may apply.
