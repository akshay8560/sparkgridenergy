VEU Air Conditioning Discounts in Victoria

Understand possible VEU discounts for efficient reverse-cycle aircon upgrades.

Under the Victorian Energy Upgrades program, eligible households and businesses may receive upfront discounts when installing approved high-efficiency reverse-cycle air conditioners.

The Victorian Government lists heating and cooling discounts for upgrades such as replacing gas room heaters, ducted gas heating, hard-wired electric heaters, old reverse-cycle units or installing eligible new systems.

Possible Upgrades

Existing SystemPossible Upgrade
Gas room heaterReverse-cycle split system
Ducted gas heaterMultiple splits, multi-split or ducted reverse-cycle
Hard-wired electric heaterReverse-cycle split system
Old reverse-cycle airconNew approved efficient reverse-cycle aircon
No old system removedLower discount may apply

Minimum Co-Payment

Minimum customer co-payments may apply under the VEU program. For many non-ducted split systems below 10 kW, the minimum co-payment starts from $200 per installed product. Larger systems, ducted systems and multi-split systems may have higher co-payment requirements.

Discounts are not guaranteed. Final eligibility, product approval, VEEC calculation, decommissioning requirements and quotation must be confirmed by the nominated VEU Accredited Provider before installation.
SparkGrid Energy assists customers with VEU upgrade enquiries and coordination. Final eligibility, discount amount, product approval, installation requirements and VEEC creation are subject to assessment and approval by the relevant VEU Accredited Provider.
